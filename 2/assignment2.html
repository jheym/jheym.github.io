Dataset

We are using the Dataset from the VAST 2022 challenge. The data includes tracking information from 1,010 participants over the course of fifteen months. It includes over 18 gigabytes of location data, financial, health, and social interactions which are timestamped every five minutes. We are interested in exploring any interesting patterns or anomalies with the dataset to better understand the behavior of the fictional town of Engagement, Ohio.

The data is available online at: https://vast-challenge.github.io/2022/

Initial Analysis Questions

  1. What are the spending habits of people around town?
  2. What can we gather about the popularity of businesses around town?
  3. Are there any patterns of participant-business activity that occur over time?

Summary

Discoveries & Insights drawn from the visualizations:
From the visualizations we created we've found that the cost of living in the city is lower than the national average when looking at rental costs, educational spending, and yearly spending on food. We've also discovered from the monthly extra budget visualization that the average disposable income has been increasing steadily over time. Discoveries & Insights made from creating the visualizations:
